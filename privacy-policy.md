# Privacy Policy for PrayerStack

Last updated: December 2025

PrayerStack is a simple prayer tracking application built by Noerdev.

## Information Collection and Use

PrayerStack does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
All prayer entries and settings are stored locally on the user's device.

The app does not require account creation and does not transmit data to external servers.

## Permissions

PrayerStack may request notification permissions in order to remind users of scheduled prayer times. These notifications are generated locally on the device.

The app does not access contacts, location, microphone, camera, or files.

## Third-Party Services

PrayerStack does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or data processors.
